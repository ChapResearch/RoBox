Challenge #8

Lazer Run!

In this challenge you will program your robot to Light Up and make a sound when its hit by an Infared (IR) Lazer!

Now you have 2 new blocks, the LED block, and the Beep block.

The LED block turns on one of the three lights on the robot, but it doesnt turn them off. So don't forget to use another LED block to turn them off when you're done! The following blocks turn on the first set of lights then turns them off a second later.


The Beep block makes the robot make a sound at a certain frequency for a certain number of seconds. The following Beep blocks plays a sound at a frequency of 260Hz for 2 seconds.


Remember to use an If block and a Repeat Forever block. Good Luck!
