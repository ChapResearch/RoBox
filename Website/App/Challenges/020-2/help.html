Challenge #2

Drive And Come Back

Congratulations! You have succesfully made your first program. Now you have moved onto challenge #2

In this challenge you will program your robot to drive away from you, turn, and come back.

This time you will be using the Wheel block to move each wheel on your robot. To get started use a Wheel right block and a Wheel left block and set their power to 100. This will make the robot move forward


Now use a wait block to tell your robot to wait for a few seconds.


Next make your wheels stop moving by using the Wheel right block and a Wheel left block and set their power to 0

Everytime you complete a new program and want your robot to run it click the button.

You might notice that this only makes your robot move forward, but we want the robot to come back. Try changing the power on the first 2 Wheel blocks and see what happens.
